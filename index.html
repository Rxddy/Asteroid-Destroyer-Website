About Me

Hi! I'm Rusanth Pirabakaran, a passionate game developer with a love for retro gaming. I have always been fascinated by the classic arcade games from the 80s and 90s, which is why I decided to create "Asteroid Destroyer," a retro-style space shooter game. I use dPython, Html, Css and Java to create both the website and the game. I'm constantly learning new techniques to improve my skills. In addition to my love for game development, I enjoy playing sports such as volleyball and playing games like Valorant. I created this website to showcase my work and connect with other gamers and developers. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or discuss gaming projects!
